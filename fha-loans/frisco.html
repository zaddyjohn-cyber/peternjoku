Down Payment Help

DPA programs for Frisco FHA buyers

TSAHC Home Sweet Texas

A 3–5% grant on your loan amount — no repayment, ever. Available across all of Collin County including Frisco. No first-time buyer requirement. Income limit ~$119,700 for Collin County in 2026. On a $450,000 Frisco home, a 4% grant = $18,000 — more than the entire 3.5% down payment.

TDHCA My First Texas Home

Up to 5% of your loan amount as a deferred second-lien, forgiven at year 3. Paired with a below-market FHA rate. For first-time buyers and veterans in Frisco. Income limits apply. Can be layered with TSAHC in some scenarios to cover both down payment and closing costs.

New Construction Notes

Frisco builders often offer their own closing cost incentives when you use their preferred lender — but those deals frequently require you to sacrifice rate or terms. I'll compare the builder's offer against FHA with TSAHC so you know which path actually saves you more money over 5 years.
