DPA programs for Rockwall FHA buyers

TSAHC Home Sweet Texas

A 3–5% grant — no repayment, ever. Available across all of Rockwall County. No first-time buyer requirement. Income limit ~$119,700 for 2026 — one of the higher limits in DFW. On a $360,000 Rockwall home, a 4% grant = $14,400, covering the full 3.5% down payment.

TDHCA My First Texas Home

Up to 5% of your loan amount as a deferred second-lien, forgiven at year 3. For first-time buyers and veterans in Rockwall. Paired with a below-market FHA rate. Income limits apply. A strong complement to TSAHC when you need both down payment and closing cost coverage.

Stacking Strategy

In some scenarios, TSAHC and TDHCA can be layered to cover both your down payment and closing costs — bringing cash-to-close close to zero. I'll show you exactly what's available for your Rockwall purchase price and income in the first call.
