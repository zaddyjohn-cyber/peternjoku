DPA programs for Terrell FHA buyers

TSAHC Home Sweet Texas

A 3–5% grant — no repayment, ever. Available across all of Kaufman County including Terrell. No first-time buyer requirement. Income limit ~$112,450 for 2026. On a $240,000 home, a 4% grant = $9,600 — covers the full 3.5% down payment with cash left for closing costs.

TDHCA My First Texas Home

Up to 5% deferred, forgiven at year 3. Paired with a below-market FHA rate. For first-time buyers and veterans in Terrell. A strong option for buyers who want both the lowest rate and the most down payment help available.

FHA vs. USDA

Terrell is USDA-eligible — 100% financing for income-qualified buyers. FHA + TSAHC is the alternative: near-zero down, no address restrictions, no USDA annual fee. I'll run both programs for your specific income and target price so you pick the one that saves the most. Call 469-545-7180.
