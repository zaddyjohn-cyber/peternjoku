Down Payment Help

DPA programs for Plano FHA buyers

TSAHC Home Sweet Texas

A 3–5% grant on your loan amount — no repayment, ever. Works with FHA loans across all of Collin County including Plano. No first-time buyer requirement. Income limit ~$119,700 for Collin County in 2026. On a $400,000 Plano home, a 5% grant covers the entire 3.5% down payment with cash left over for closing costs.

TDHCA My First Texas Home

Up to 5% of your loan amount as a deferred second-lien, forgiven at year 3. Pairs with a below-market FHA first mortgage rate. For first-time buyers and veterans. Income limits apply. A strong alternative or complement to TSAHC for Plano buyers who want the lowest possible payment.

Stacking Options

In some scenarios TSAHC and TDHCA can be combined to cover both down payment and closing costs — bringing your cash-to-close close to zero. The best combination depends on your income, credit score, and first-time buyer status. I'll map this out for you in the first call.
