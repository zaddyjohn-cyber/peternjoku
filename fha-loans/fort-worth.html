Down Payment Help

DPA programs for Fort Worth FHA buyers

Fort Worth HAP — up to $25,000

The Fort Worth Homebuyer Assistance Program provides up to $25,000 in forgivable down payment and closing cost help. Forgiven over 5 years, no monthly payments on the assistance. Must purchase within Fort Worth city limits. First-time buyer required. Income limits apply (~$97,200 for Tarrant County in 2026).

TSAHC Home Sweet Texas

A 3–5% grant on your loan amount — no repayment ever. Works for all of Texas, no city-limits restriction. Ideal if your Fort Worth property falls just outside HAP's eligible zone, or if HAP funding is temporarily exhausted. No first-time buyer requirement.

TDHCA My First Texas Home

Up to 5% deferred second-lien assistance, forgiven at year 3. Paired with a below-market FHA rate through TDHCA. For first-time buyers and Texas veterans across all of Fort Worth. A strong option when layering with HAP isn't possible.
