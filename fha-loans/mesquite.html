Down Payment Help

DPA programs available for Mesquite FHA buyers

TSAHC Home Sweet Texas

A 3–5% grant on your loan amount — no repayment, ever. Works with FHA loans for buyers across Texas, including all of Mesquite. No first-time buyer requirement. Income limits apply (~$97,200 for Dallas County in 2026). One of the cleanest DPA programs available.

Dallas DHAP

Up to $60,000 in forgivable down payment and closing cost assistance for qualifying addresses. Forgiven over 5 years. First-time buyer required. Some Mesquite properties fall within the eligible boundary — I'll check your specific address before you get your hopes up.

TDHCA My First Texas Home

Up to 5% of your loan amount as a deferred second-lien, forgiven at year 3. Pairs with a below-market FHA first mortgage rate. For first-time buyers and veterans. Income limits apply. A strong fallback if DHAP isn't available for your address.
