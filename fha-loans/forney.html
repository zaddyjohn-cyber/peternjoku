DPA programs for Forney FHA buyers

TSAHC Home Sweet Texas

A 3–5% grant on your loan amount — no repayment, ever. Available across all of Kaufman County including Forney. No first-time buyer requirement. Income limit ~$112,450 for Kaufman County in 2026. On a $275,000 Forney home, a 4% grant = $11,000 — covering the full 3.5% down payment with cash toward closing costs.

TDHCA My First Texas Home

Up to 5% deferred assistance, forgiven at year 3. Paired with a below-market FHA rate. For first-time buyers and veterans in Forney. Income limits apply. A strong complement to TSAHC when you want the lowest possible rate and need closing cost coverage too.
