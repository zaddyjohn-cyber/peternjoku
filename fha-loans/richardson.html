Down Payment Help

DPA programs for Richardson FHA buyers

TSAHC Home Sweet Texas

A 3–5% grant — no repayment, ever. Available across all of Richardson. No first-time buyer requirement. Income limit ~$97,200 for Dallas County or ~$119,700 for Collin County portions of Richardson (2026). This is available to every Richardson buyer regardless of which side of the county line their home is on.

Dallas DHAP

Up to $60,000 forgivable over 5 years. Available for addresses within the City of Dallas boundary — some Richardson addresses qualify. First-time buyer required. I'll check your specific address before including this in your plan. When it's available, it's the strongest DPA in DFW.

TDHCA My First Texas Home

Up to 5% of your loan amount as a deferred second-lien, forgiven at year 3. Paired with a below-market FHA rate. For first-time buyers and veterans throughout Richardson. A strong fallback or complement when Dallas DHAP isn't available for your address.
