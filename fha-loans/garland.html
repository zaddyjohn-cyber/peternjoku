Down Payment Help

FHA + DPA: how Garland buyers cover the down payment

Dallas DHAP

Up to $60,000 in forgivable down payment and closing cost assistance for eligible buyers. Forgiven over 5 years — no monthly payments on the assistance. Income limits apply. First-time buyer required. I'll tell you upfront if your Garland address qualifies.

TSAHC Home Sweet Texas

A 3–5% grant on the loan amount — no repayment ever required. Works with FHA loans for buyers across all of Texas, including all of Garland. No first-time buyer requirement. Income limits apply (~$97,200 for Dallas/Tarrant County in 2026).

TDHCA My First Texas Home

Up to 5% of the loan in deferred second-lien assistance, forgiven at year 3. Combined with a competitive FHA first mortgage rate. Available to first-time buyers and veterans in Garland. Income limits apply.

These programs can't always be combined with each other, but they can be combined with FHA. I'll match you to the best combination based on your income, credit, and the home's address. Text me at 469-545-7180 and I'll run your numbers same day.
