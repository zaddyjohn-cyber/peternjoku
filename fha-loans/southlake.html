The right loan for your Southlake target price

FHA — Entry-Level Southlake

For Southlake homes priced $450,000–$524,225 — townhomes, smaller SFRs — FHA at 3.5% down is a valid option. TSAHC is available for buyers under ~$97,200 income. Best for 580–679 credit or buyers who want the lowest possible down payment on the entry-level end of the Southlake market.

Conventional — The Mainstream Path

For Southlake homes above $524,225, conventional is standard. Higher loan limits, lower mortgage insurance with strong credit (720+), and no FHA MIP structure. Typically requires 5–20% down. I'll find the best conventional rate for your price, credit, and down payment. Call 469-545-7180.

VA — Best for Eligible Veterans

Since January 2020, VA loans have no loan limit for eligible veterans with full entitlement. A Southlake home at $750,000 or $900,000 is fully financeable with $0 down and no monthly mortgage insurance. If you're a veteran, this is almost always the most powerful Southlake loan option.
