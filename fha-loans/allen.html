Down Payment Help

DPA programs for Allen FHA buyers

TSAHC Home Sweet Texas

A 3–5% grant on your loan amount — no repayment, ever. Works with FHA loans across all of Collin County including Allen. No first-time buyer requirement. Income limit ~$119,700 for Collin County in 2026. This is typically the first program I reach for with Allen buyers.

TDHCA My First Texas Home

Up to 5% of your loan amount as a deferred second-lien, forgiven at year 3. Pairs with a below-market FHA rate. For first-time buyers and veterans. Income limits apply. A strong option when stacked with an FHA loan for Allen buyers who want the lowest possible monthly payment.

Combining Programs

In some cases TSAHC and TDHCA assistance can be layered to cover both your down payment and closing costs. The right combination depends on your income, credit, and whether you're a first-time buyer. I'll identify the best stack for your Allen purchase in the first conversation.
