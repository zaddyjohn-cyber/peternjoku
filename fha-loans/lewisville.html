DPA programs for Lewisville FHA buyers

TSAHC Home Sweet Texas

A 3–5% grant — no repayment, ever. Available across Denton County. Income limit ~$119,700 for 2026. No first-time buyer requirement. On a $310,000 Lewisville home, a 4% grant = $12,400, covering the full down payment and contributing to closing costs. One of the best DPA programs in DFW.

TDHCA My First Texas Home

Up to 5% deferred, forgiven at year 3. Paired with a below-market FHA rate. For first-time buyers and veterans in Lewisville. Pairs well with TSAHC when you need both a lower rate and maximum down payment coverage.

Denton County Advantage

Denton County's income limit (~$119,700) is $22,500 higher than Dallas County's limit. That means more Lewisville buyers qualify for TSAHC than their Dallas or Mesquite counterparts. If you've been told you make too much for DPA elsewhere, call me — Lewisville may qualify.
