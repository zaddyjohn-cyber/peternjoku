DPA programs for Mansfield FHA buyers

TSAHC Home Sweet Texas

A 3–5% grant — no repayment, ever. Available across all of Tarrant County including Mansfield. Income limit ~$97,200 for 2026. On a $340,000 home, a 4% grant = $13,600 — covering the $11,900 down payment and contributing toward closing costs.

TDHCA My First Texas Home

Up to 5% deferred, forgiven at year 3. Paired with a below-market FHA rate. For first-time buyers and veterans in Mansfield. Ideal for buyers who need both a lower rate and maximum upfront coverage in Mansfield's growing market.
