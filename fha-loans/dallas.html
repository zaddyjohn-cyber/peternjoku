FHA + Dallas DHAP

Dallas has one of the strongest DPA programs in Texas

What makes Dallas unique for FHA buyers is the Dallas Homebuyer Assistance Program — DHAP. It offers up to $60,000 in forgivable down payment and closing cost assistance for eligible buyers purchasing within Dallas city limits. That's far more than most DPA programs across DFW, and it's fully compatible with FHA loans.

In practice, this means a qualifying Dallas buyer can purchase a home in Oak Cliff, Pleasant Grove, South Dallas, or East Dallas with very little out of pocket. On a $250,000 home with 3.5% FHA down payment ($8,750) and $60,000 DHAP assistance, you're looking at a deal where the assistance covers far more than your down payment and closing costs combined.

Not every buyer or property qualifies — income limits apply, the property must be within Dallas city limits, and first-time buyer status is required. I'll check all of this for you before we go further.

Down Payment Help

DPA programs for Dallas FHA buyers

Dallas DHAP — up to $60,000

The Dallas Homebuyer Assistance Program is the most generous DPA in DFW. Up to $60,000 in forgivable down payment and closing cost assistance — forgiven over 5 years, no monthly payments on the assistance. Must purchase within Dallas city limits. First-time buyer required. Income limits apply (~$97,200 for a household in 2026).

TSAHC Home Sweet Texas

A 3–5% grant based on your loan amount — no repayment ever. Statewide program, no city-limits restriction. Works as a backup if your property is just outside Dallas DHAP's boundary, or if DHAP funding is temporarily paused. No first-time buyer requirement.

TDHCA My First Texas Home

Up to 5% of the loan amount as a deferred second-lien, forgiven at year 3. Pairs with a below-market FHA rate through TDHCA. For first-time buyers and Texas veterans. A solid option when DHAP eligibility is borderline or unavailable.
