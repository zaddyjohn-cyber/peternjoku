How USDA Works in Rockwall

Not every street qualifies — but many do

Rockwall is one of the most desirable counties in DFW, and USDA eligibility maps are drawn at the address level — not the city level. This means some neighborhoods within Rockwall city limits fall inside the eligible zone, while others do not. Communities like Royse City (within Rockwall County) are reliably eligible; portions of Heath, McLendon-Chisholm, and other unincorporated areas are also typically eligible.

The good news for Rockwall County buyers is the income limit: approximately $119,700 for a household of 1–4 people. This is one of the highest USDA income limits in DFW, reflecting the county's higher median incomes. Many buyers who would be over the limit in Dallas or Kaufman County still qualify here.
