Why USDA in Terrell?

The most affordable entry point in east DFW — with $0 down

Terrell sits about 30 miles east of downtown Dallas on I-20, in the heart of Kaufman County. It has a quiet, small-city feel with a growing community, and home prices that are still some of the lowest in the DFW metro — typically between $170,000 and $280,000. That price range paired with USDA's $0 down financing creates one of the lowest barriers to homeownership in the entire region.

For buyers who are tired of watching their rent money disappear but haven't been able to save a down payment, Terrell is a real path forward. A qualified buyer can stop renting and start building equity — often with a monthly mortgage payment similar to or lower than their current rent.
