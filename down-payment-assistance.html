Down Payment Assistance

Down Payment Assistance Options for Texas Homebuyers

The down payment is often the biggest hurdle for first-time buyers. Texas offers several assistance programs and grant opportunities — we will help you understand which may fit.

Check Your OptionsTalk to us
What is DPA?

Help with what you bring to the table

Down payment assistance can come in several forms — grants, low-interest second liens, or forgivable loans — designed to reduce your out-of-pocket costs at closing.

Statewide programs

Texas-administered programs help eligible buyers reduce their down payment or closing costs.

Local & city programs

Cities and counties may offer their own assistance for income-qualified buyers in specific areas.

Loan program features

Some loan programs (FHA, VA, USDA) reduce or eliminate down payment requirements for qualified borrowers.

How DPA works

Stacking assistance with your loan

You may qualify if…

  • Your household income falls within program limits
  • You’re buying in a qualifying Texas area
  • You meet credit and loan requirements
  • You complete required homebuyer education

What it can cover

  • All or part of your down payment
  • Some closing costs
  • Pairings with FHA, VA, USDA, or Conventional
  • Reduced upfront cost without changing loan structure
