Who Qualifies for a VA Loan in Southlake?

Veterans

Honorably discharged veterans of any branch qualify. Service length requirements vary: 90 days wartime or 181 days peacetime active duty.

Active Duty

Service members with 90+ days of continuous active duty qualify. VA loans are commonly used for PCS moves to the DFW area.

Guard & Reserve

National Guard and Reserve members with 6+ years of service or 90+ days of active-duty orders (Title 10) qualify.
