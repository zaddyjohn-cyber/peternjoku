Who Qualifies

VA loan eligibility for McKinney buyers

Veterans

Honorably discharged veterans with 90+ days active duty (wartime) or 181+ days (peacetime). If you served and were honorably discharged, you almost certainly qualify. I'll pull your COE in minutes.

Active Duty Service Members

Currently serving with at least 90 continuous days. McKinney is a popular destination for active-duty families from nearby installations — you can use VA benefits before separation.

National Guard & Reserve

Guard and Reserve members with 6+ years of service, or activated under federal Title 10 orders for 90+ days. Call me and I'll verify your specific eligibility before you apply.
