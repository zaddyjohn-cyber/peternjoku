Who Qualifies

VA loan eligibility for Plano buyers

Veterans

Honorably discharged veterans with 90+ days active duty (wartime) or 181+ days (peacetime). If you served and were honorably discharged, you almost certainly qualify. I'll confirm your COE in minutes.

Active Duty Service Members

Currently serving with at least 90 continuous days. Many active-duty families buy in Plano for the school district and corporate employer access — you can use VA benefits before you separate.

National Guard & Reserve

Guard and Reserve members who have served 6+ years, or activated under federal Title 10 orders for 90+ days. Corporate Plano is a natural fit for Guard members who hold civilian tech jobs here.
