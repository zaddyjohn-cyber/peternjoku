VA + Dallas DHAP

Dallas veterans have a powerful two-program combination available

VA loans already eliminate the down payment — but Dallas adds another layer for qualifying veterans. The Dallas Homebuyer Assistance Program (DHAP) provides up to $60,000 in forgivable assistance for eligible first-time buyers purchasing within Dallas city limits. Because VA takes care of the down payment, DHAP funds can go almost entirely toward closing costs — appraisal, title, prepaid taxes and insurance, lender fees.

The result for a qualifying Dallas veteran: a home purchased with $0 down and $0 in closing costs. Not every veteran or property qualifies — DHAP requires first-time buyer status, income limits, and a property within Dallas city limits — but when it works, it's one of the strongest purchase scenarios available anywhere in DFW. I'll verify both your VA entitlement and DHAP eligibility before we go further.

Who Qualifies

VA eligibility for Dallas homebuyers

Veterans

Honorably discharged veterans who meet minimum active-duty service requirements. Most veterans who served 90+ days during wartime or 181+ days during peacetime qualify. Dallas has a large veteran population — the VA Medical Center at 4500 S. Lancaster Road serves thousands in the area.

Active Duty

Currently serving with at least 90 continuous days of active duty. Dallas-area active-duty personnel connected to NAS JRB Fort Worth, Naval Recruiting District Dallas, or other installations can use their VA benefit to purchase in Dallas today.

Surviving Spouses

Un-remarried surviving spouses of veterans who died in service or from a service-connected disability may be eligible for VA loan benefits. I'll verify eligibility and walk through the COE process with you. Call 469-545-7180.
