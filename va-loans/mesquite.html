Who Qualifies

VA loan eligibility for Mesquite buyers

Veterans

Honorably discharged veterans who meet minimum service requirements. Generally 90+ days active duty during wartime or 181+ days peacetime. If you served and were honorably discharged, you almost certainly qualify. I'll confirm your COE in minutes.

Active Duty Service Members

Currently serving with at least 90 continuous days. You don't have to wait until discharge — you can use VA benefits now. Many active-duty members choose Mesquite for its affordability and proximity to Dallas employment corridors.

National Guard & Reserve

Guard and Reserve members who have served 6+ years, or activated under federal Title 10 orders for 90+ days. The DFW area has a significant Guard and Reserve presence. Call me and I'll check your specific eligibility.
