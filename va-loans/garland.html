Who Qualifies

VA loan eligibility for Garland buyers

Veterans

Honorably discharged veterans who meet minimum service requirements. Generally 90+ days of active duty during wartime, or 181+ days during peacetime. If you served and were honorably discharged, you almost certainly qualify. I'll confirm your COE in minutes.

Active Duty Service Members

Currently serving on active duty with at least 90 continuous days of service. Can use VA benefits now — you don't have to wait until discharge. Helpful for members stationed near Garland or commuting to Dallas-area installations.

National Guard & Reserve

Guard and Reserve members who have served 6+ years, or who were activated under federal Title 10 orders for 90+ days. The Garland area has a significant Guard presence — if you've served in the Texas National Guard, call me and I'll check your eligibility.
