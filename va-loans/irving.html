VA IRRRL

Already have a VA loan in Irving? The IRRRL can lower your rate.

The VA Interest Rate Reduction Refinance Loan — IRRRL, often called the VA Streamline Refinance — is one of the simplest refinance options available. If you already have a VA loan on your Irving home and current rates are lower than when you closed, an IRRRL can reduce your monthly payment with minimal paperwork and no appraisal required in most cases.

There's no income verification, no new Certificate of Eligibility required, and the new loan stays VA-backed. The funding fee on an IRRRL is just 0.5%, and it can be rolled into the loan. If you're paying a rate above today's market, this is worth a five-minute conversation.

VA IRRRL — quick facts

  • Must already have a VA loan on the property
  • No appraisal required in most cases
  • No income verification required
  • Funding fee just 0.5%
  • New rate must be lower than current rate
