VA vs USDA in Royse City

Both offer $0 down — VA wins on monthly cost for veterans

Royse City is one of the few DFW communities where both VA and USDA loans are available, since most Royse City addresses qualify for USDA rural financing. If you're a veteran, you get to choose — and VA typically wins. USDA charges a 0.35% annual mortgage guarantee fee for the life of the loan; VA has no equivalent monthly charge. On a $300,000 Royse City home, that's $1,050/year in USDA fees you'd avoid with VA.

VA also has no income limit and is not restricted to USDA-eligible addresses. For eligible veterans buying in Royse City, VA is almost always the better tool.

  • $0 down — same as USDA, but no annual fee
  • No monthly PMI or guarantee fee
  • No income limits (unlike USDA)
  • No address-eligibility restriction
  • Flexible credit (580+)
  • Reusable benefit after payoff
