Who Qualifies

VA loan eligibility for Lewisville buyers

Veterans

Honorably discharged veterans with 90+ days active duty (wartime) or 181+ days (peacetime). If you served and were honorably discharged, you almost certainly qualify. I'll pull your COE in minutes.

Active Duty Service Members

Currently serving with at least 90 continuous days. Lewisville's proximity to DFW Airport and major employment corridors makes it practical for active-duty families — use VA benefits before separation.

National Guard & Reserve

Guard and Reserve members with 6+ years of service, or activated under federal Title 10 orders for 90+ days. Call me and I'll verify your eligibility before you apply.
