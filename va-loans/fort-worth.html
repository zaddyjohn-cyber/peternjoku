NAS JRB Fort Worth and a city built around service

VA + Fort Worth HAP

Stacking VA with Fort Worth's homebuyer assistance

Since VA loans eliminate the down payment, the Fort Worth HAP program's $25,000 in forgivable assistance can be directed almost entirely toward closing costs — appraisal, title, prepaid taxes and insurance, and lender fees. This leaves many Fort Worth VA buyers getting to the closing table with virtually no out-of-pocket expense at all.

HAP eligibility requires purchasing within Fort Worth city limits, meeting income limits, and being a first-time buyer. Not every VA buyer qualifies, but it's worth checking before we proceed. I'll verify both your VA entitlement and your HAP eligibility in the same conversation.
