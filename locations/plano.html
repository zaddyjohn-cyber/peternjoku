What type of mortgage is most common for Plano, TX homebuyers?
Conventional loans are the most common in Plano due to higher home prices and strong buyer credit profiles. However, FHA loans are used for buyers with lower down payments, and VA loans are popular with Plano's active military and veteran community. I'm Bond Peter Njoku (NMLS #2670329) and I review all options and recommend the best fit for your situation.
Can I get mortgage pre-approval for a Plano, TX home quickly?
Yes. I'm Bond Peter Njoku, and I typically deliver pre-approval decisions within 24–48 hours of receiving documents — pay stubs, W-2s, bank statements, and a valid ID. Yes. Plano buyers may qualify for TSAHC Home Sweet Texas (3–5% grant, no repayment), TDHCA My First Texas Home (up to 5% DPA for first-timers and veterans), and other Collin County programs.
