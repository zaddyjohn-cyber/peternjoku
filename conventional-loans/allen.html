Understanding PMI

How PMI works — and when it goes away in Allen TX

PMI (private mortgage insurance) on a conventional loan is not permanent — that's the key difference from FHA's MIP. On a conventional loan, PMI cancels in two ways:

  • Automatic cancellation — when your balance reaches 78% of the original purchase price (by scheduled amortization)
  • Requested cancellation — at 80% LTV if you request it in writing and your payment history is clean
  • Appraisal-based cancellation — if appreciation pushes your LTV below 80%, you can request cancellation with a new appraisal

Allen's consistent appreciation history means many buyers hit that 80% threshold faster than their amortization schedule predicts — especially buyers who buy in rising comps neighborhoods.
