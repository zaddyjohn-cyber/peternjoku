Financing in Southlake

Understanding conforming vs. jumbo in Southlake's market

Southlake's price range goes well beyond conventional's $806,500 conforming limit. Here's how to think about financing options by price range:

  • Under $806,500: conventional financing available (620+ credit, 5–20% down)
  • Over $806,500: jumbo financing (typically 720+ credit, 15–20% down)
  • Veterans: VA loans have no loan limit — $0 down regardless of price
  • FHA max: $524,225 — covers very little of Southlake's market
  • Conventional PMI: best rates at 740+ credit, cancels at 78% LTV

Most Southlake buyers I work with are either targeting the $600,000–$806,500 conforming range with conventional, or are veterans leveraging VA's no-limit benefit. I'll assess your target price and recommend the right program.
