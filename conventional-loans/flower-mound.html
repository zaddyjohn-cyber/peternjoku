Why Conventional in Flower Mound?

Flower Mound's price range is built for conventional — not FHA

Flower Mound's median home price is well above FHA's Denton County loan limit of $524,225. Buyers targeting $400,000–$700,000 homes have two realistic options: conventional (up to $806,500 conforming limit) or VA (no loan limit for veterans). FHA is only an option for the entry-level end of Flower Mound's market.

Most Flower Mound buyers I work with have 700–760+ credit, substantial down payments, and dual-income households. Conventional delivers the best pricing at these profiles — competitive rates, no upfront MIP, lower PMI that cancels, and access to the full Flower Mound price range up to $806,500.
