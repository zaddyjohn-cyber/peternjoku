Why Conventional in Fort Worth?

Fort Worth's diverse market rewards conventional buyers with strong credit

Fort Worth is one of the fastest-growing cities in the US, with neighborhoods spanning every price point. For move-up buyers in Aledo, Benbrook, or near TCU — and for professionals working in the medical district or Alliance corridor — conventional financing at 680+ credit delivers the best monthly economics: lower PMI than FHA, no upfront insurance premium, and PMI that eventually cancels as home equity grows.
