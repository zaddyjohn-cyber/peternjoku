Why Conventional in Forney?

When conventional beats USDA in Forney TX

Forney is known for its USDA eligibility — $0 down with no private mortgage insurance (just a small annual guarantee fee). But USDA has income limits (~$112,450 for Kaufman County households) and geographic restrictions that not every Forney address meets. Buyers who've moved past those limits, or who want to put 5–10% down to accelerate equity, find conventional is the right next step.

Conventional PMI at 680+ credit is lower than USDA's annual guarantee fee in some scenarios, and conventional PMI cancels at 78% LTV — the USDA fee doesn't. New-construction buyers also benefit from conventional's flexibility with builder incentive programs.

  • No income limits (USDA caps ~$112,450 in Kaufman County)
  • PMI cancels at 78% LTV — USDA fee never cancels
  • No geographic address restriction
  • 2026 Kaufman County loan limit: $806,500
  • Works with new-construction builder incentives
