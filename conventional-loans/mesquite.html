Why Conventional in Mesquite?

Lower monthly cost for Mesquite buyers with solid credit

Mesquite is one of Dallas County's most affordable markets — $200,000–$340,000 homes with easy I-30 and I-635 access. FHA is common here, especially for first-time buyers, but buyers who've rebuilt credit above 680 often find conventional delivers a noticeably better monthly payment. No 1.75% upfront MIP, lower monthly PMI, and PMI that eventually cancels.

Many Mesquite buyers I work with are transitioning from FHA-eligible first homes to conventional-eligible purchases as their credit and income have grown. If you've been told "just go FHA" without a side-by-side comparison, call me — the difference is often $100–$150/month at 680+ credit.
