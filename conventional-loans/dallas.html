Why Conventional in Dallas?

Dallas's wide price range is built for conventional financing

Dallas neighborhoods span every price point in DFW — Lower Greenville, Lakewood, Oak Cliff, East Dallas, Deep Ellum, and dozens of established neighborhoods where homes sell $280,000–$600,000. The $806,500 Dallas County conforming limit covers virtually every standard Dallas purchase.
