Financing in Prosper

Understanding conforming vs. jumbo in Prosper's market

Prosper's price range spans from entry-level new construction around $450,000 all the way past $1M. Conventional covers a large share of that range — here's how the tiers break down:

  • Under $806,500: conventional financing available (620+ credit, 5–20% down)
  • Over $806,500: jumbo financing (typically 720+ credit, 15–20% down)
  • Veterans: VA loans have no loan limit — $0 down, no monthly PMI
  • FHA max: $524,225 — covers only entry-level Prosper new construction
  • TSAHC DPA: income limit ~$119,700 for Collin County — applies at conventional price points

Most Prosper buyers I work with are targeting the $500,000–$806,500 sweet spot where conventional delivers the best combination of rate, PMI cancellation, and low upfront cost. For larger floor plans above $806,500, I discuss jumbo options or — for veterans — VA with no loan limit.
