Bond Peter Njoku, NMLS #2670329, helps Texas families understand their financing options and make informed homeownership decisions. With experience in mortgage processing and loan origination, Bond guides clients from application through closing — translating mortgage jargon into plain language so you can make the right call for your family.

Working with Mortgage Funding Solutions, Bond is licensed to help Texas borrowers compare FHA, VA, USDA, Conventional, and refinance options. Whether you’re a first-time buyer in Garland, a Veteran in Plano, a growing family in Frisco, or a homeowner thinking about a refinance, Bond brings the same approach to every file: clear communication, honest guidance, and steady support to the closing table.
