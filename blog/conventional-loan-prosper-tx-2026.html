Conventional Loan Requirements in Prosper TX for 2026

Conventional loans follow Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac guidelines statewide. Here's what you need to qualify in Prosper in 2026:

  • Credit score: 620 minimum; 680+ recommended for the best pricing
  • Down payment: 3% minimum (HomeReady/Home Possible); 10%+ common on new-construction purchases
  • Debt-to-income ratio: Generally 43–45% max; up to 50% with compensating factors
  • Employment: 2 years of verifiable employment or self-employment history
  • Loan amount: Up to $806,500 (2026 conforming limit covering Prosper)
  • Property type: Single-family, new construction, and master-planned community homes

New construction contracts in Prosper often come with a builder's preferred lender offering incentives — I regularly compare those against my own conventional rates so you know which option actually saves you more.

2026 Conventional Loan Limits in Prosper TX

Prosper spans Collin and Denton counties, both of which carry a 2026 conforming loan limit of $806,500 for a single-family home. That covers the large majority of Prosper purchases, including most new-construction inventory, though top-tier custom builds can push into jumbo territory.

For more Prosper-specific details, see my Prosper conventional loans page, the main conventional loans guide, or my Prosper mortgage page.

Conventional Loans and New Construction in Prosper TX

Prosper's growth is almost entirely new-construction driven, which changes how I approach financing compared to resale-heavy markets. My general rule of thumb for 2026:

  • Go conventional if: Credit score 680+, putting down 5% or more, want the flexibility to shop any lender
  • Compare builder incentives carefully: Builder-preferred lenders sometimes offer rate buydowns, but often at a higher base rate — I'll show you the real numbers
  • Lock your rate early: New-construction closings can run 6–12 months out, so I help you plan the right rate-lock strategy

Timing your rate lock correctly on a new-construction contract can save thousands over the life of the loan. Call or text me at 469-545-7180 and I'll walk through your builder's timeline with you.
