Conventional Loan Requirements in Rockwall TX for 2026

Conventional loans follow Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac guidelines statewide. Here's what you need to qualify in Rockwall in 2026:

  • Credit score: 620 minimum; 680+ recommended for the best pricing
  • Down payment: 3% minimum (HomeReady/Home Possible); 10%+ common on lake-area purchases
  • Debt-to-income ratio: Generally 43–45% max; up to 50% with compensating factors
  • Employment: 2 years of verifiable employment or self-employment history
  • Loan amount: Up to $806,500 (Rockwall County conforming limit, 2026)
  • Property type: Single-family, waterfront properties, townhomes, and select condos

Lake-adjacent properties in Rockwall sometimes come with condition quirks — older docks, unique lot configurations — that conventional financing handles more flexibly than FHA's stricter appraisal standards.

Conventional vs. FHA vs. USDA in Rockwall TX

Rockwall is one of the few DFW cities where I regularly compare all three loan types for a single buyer. My general rule of thumb for 2026:

  • Go conventional if: Credit score 680+, buying inside city limits, putting down 5% or more
  • Consider FHA if: Credit score 580–639, minimal down payment, buying inside city limits
  • Consider USDA if: Your specific address falls in a USDA-eligible rural area just outside Rockwall proper — zero down beats everything

Address eligibility is the deciding factor for USDA, so I always check the specific parcel before ruling it out. Call or text me at 469-545-7180 with the address and I'll tell you within minutes which programs apply.
