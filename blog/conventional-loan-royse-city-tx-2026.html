Conventional Loan Requirements in Royse City TX for 2026

Conventional loans follow Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac guidelines statewide. Here's what you need to qualify in Royse City in 2026:

  • Credit score: 620 minimum; 680+ recommended for the best pricing
  • Down payment: 3% minimum (HomeReady/Home Possible)
  • Debt-to-income ratio: Generally 43–45% max; up to 50% with compensating factors
  • Employment: 2 years of verifiable employment or self-employment history
  • Loan amount: Up to $806,500 (2026 conforming limit covering Royse City)
  • Property type: Single-family, new construction, and rural-adjacent properties

Unlike USDA, conventional financing has no income cap and no restriction on where the property sits, which makes it the fallback option for buyers who love a specific Royse City neighborhood that falls just outside the USDA eligibility map.

Conventional vs. USDA vs. FHA in Royse City TX

Royse City is one of the DFW towns where I compare all three loan programs most often for a single buyer. My general rule of thumb for 2026:

  • Go USDA if: Your specific address falls in an eligible rural area and your household income is under the county limit — zero down usually wins
  • Go conventional if: Your income exceeds USDA limits, or your address falls outside the eligible map, and your credit is 680+
  • Consider FHA if: Credit score 580–639 and USDA isn't an option for your address or income

Address and income eligibility drive the decision more than anything else, so I always check both before recommending a program. Call or text me at 469-545-7180 with your target address and I'll tell you within minutes which programs apply.
