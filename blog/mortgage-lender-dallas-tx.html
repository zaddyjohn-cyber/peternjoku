Down Payment Assistance for Dallas TX Buyers in 2026

One of the biggest advantages I bring to Dallas first-time buyers is knowledge of the down payment assistance programs available in 2026 — and how to stack them correctly. Dallas has some of the most generous DPA programs in Texas:

  • Dallas DHAP (Dallas Homebuyer Assistance Program): Up to $60,000 in forgivable assistance over 5 years. For first-time buyers in Dallas city limits with income at or below 80% AMI. This is the most generous DPA in DFW and I've helped many buyers use it.
  • TSAHC Home Sweet Texas: 3–5% of the loan amount as a grant — no repayment ever. Available statewide including Dallas County. Pairs with FHA, VA, USDA, and conventional.
  • TDHCA My First Texas Home: Up to 5% deferred, forgiven after 3 years. First-time buyer or 3-year non-homeowner requirement.

Many Dallas buyers I work with combine a DPA grant with an FHA loan to reduce out-of-pocket costs to near zero at closing. See my full down payment assistance page for details on eligibility and how to apply.

What to Expect When Working With Me as Your Dallas Mortgage Lender

Here's how the process works from our first conversation to your closing day:

  1. Free consultation (Day 1): We talk through your situation — income, credit, down payment, target price range, and timeline. I tell you honestly what programs you qualify for and what your estimated payment looks like.
  2. Pre-approval (Day 1–2): You submit your documents (pay stubs, W-2s, bank statements, ID). I pull credit and issue a pre-approval letter. This is what gets you taken seriously by Dallas sellers and their agents.
  3. Home search: You shop with your realtor. I stay available to answer lender questions, adjust your pre-approval for different price points, and provide quick qualification letters for specific properties.
  4. Under contract: Once you have an accepted offer, I open your loan file, order the appraisal, and submit to underwriting. I manage the lender side so your realtor can manage the contract side.
  5. Clear to close: Underwriting approves your file. I coordinate the closing disclosure and confirm your final numbers at least 3 days before closing per federal requirements.
  6. Closing day: You sign at a Dallas-area title company. I'm available by phone if any last-minute questions come up during signing.

Throughout this process you have my direct cell — call or text me at 469-545-7180 any time. I've closed loans in Dallas, Mesquite, Garland, Irving, and across the entire DFW metroplex, and I'm committed to making your experience smooth from start to finish.
