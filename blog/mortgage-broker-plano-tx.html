Loan Programs for Plano TX Buyers in 2026

Plano's price range and buyer profile means conventional loans dominate, but I work with the full spectrum:

  • Conventional loans: 620+ FICO, 3–20% down, Collin County limit $806,500. Best for buyers with 680+ credit and 5%+ down who want no lifetime mortgage insurance.
  • FHA loans: 580+ FICO, 3.5% down. Useful for first-time Plano buyers with lower credit or limited down payment. FHA loan limit in Collin County is $524,225 for 2026.
  • VA loans: $0 down for eligible veterans, no PMI ever. I serve Plano veterans and active duty near NAS JRB Fort Worth and local Guard units.
  • Jumbo conventional: For Plano homes above $806,500 — West Plano luxury homes often fall here. Strong credit and larger down payment required.
  • Down payment assistance: TSAHC and TDHCA grants stack with FHA and conventional for Plano first-time buyers under income limits.

Most Plano move-up buyers I work with use conventional — typically 10–20% down, 700+ credit, targeting $450K–$650K homes.
