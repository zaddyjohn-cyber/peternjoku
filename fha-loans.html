FHA Home Loans

FHA Loans in Texas

FHA loans are popular among first-time buyers because they offer flexible qualification requirements and a low down payment option for qualified borrowers.

Why FHA?

Designed to make homeownership accessible

FHA loans are insured by the Federal Housing Administration, which allows lenders to offer flexible terms to a wider group of buyers. If your credit isn’t perfect or your down payment savings are limited, FHA is often the first program worth exploring.

  • Down payments as low as 3.5%
  • Flexible credit guidelines
  • Competitive rates for qualified borrowers
  • Gift funds may be accepted toward the down payment
  • Ideal for first-time buyers and borrowers rebuilding credit

FAQ

Quick FHA questions

What credit score is needed for an FHA loan?
Credit guidelines for FHA are more flexible than many programs, but specific minimums depend on the lender and your full profile. We will review your situation and explain what may qualify.
Can family help with the down payment?
Gift funds from eligible sources may be accepted toward an FHA down payment. Documentation requirements apply.
Does FHA require mortgage insurance?
FHA loans include mortgage insurance premiums. We will explain how that affects your monthly payment and overall cost.
Can I use FHA for a refinance?
Yes — FHA offers refinance options for eligible homeowners. Reach out to review what fits your goals.
