The 10-Step Roadmap

From budget to keys

Review your budget

Look at your real monthly numbers — income, fixed bills, savings goals. A comfortable mortgage payment leaves room for life.

Check your credit

Pull your reports and look for errors. Strong credit can mean stronger loan terms — but you don’t need perfect credit to buy.

Explore mortgage options

FHA, VA, USDA, Conventional — we will compare what fits your credit, savings, and target neighborhood.

Get pre-approved

Pre-approval gives you a real budget and a letter that helps your offer stand out to Texas sellers.

Shop for homes

Tour, take notes, compare. Stay close to your comfortable monthly payment — not just the max number on a letter.

Make an offer

Your Realtor and Bond work together to put together a strong offer based on your pre-approval and the local market.

Inspection and appraisal

A licensed inspector reviews the property. The lender orders an appraisal to confirm value.

Underwriting

Your full file is reviewed in detail. We keep you updated and helps with any documents requested.

Closing

Sign your final paperwork, fund your loan, and officially become a homeowner.

Move into your home

The fun part. We stay available after closing for any future financing questions.

First-Time Buyer Tips

Three rules to keep in mind

Buy what fits your life

Just because you’re approved for a higher amount doesn’t mean you should spend it.

Ask questions early

The earlier you ask, the more options you have. Nothing is too basic — we will explain it.

Avoid big credit moves

Don’t open new credit lines or make large purchases before closing — it can affect your loan.

Have a first-home question?

Bond Peter has answered a lot of them. There’s no question too small.
