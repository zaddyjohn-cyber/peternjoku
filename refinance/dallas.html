Texas Cash-Out Refinance

What Dallas homeowners need to know about Section 50(a)(6)

Texas has its own constitutional rules for cash-out refinancing — stricter than any other state. Every Dallas homeowner considering a cash-out refi needs to understand these before applying.

  • Maximum 80% LTV — you can only borrow up to 80% of appraised value, regardless of credit or income
  • Primary residence only — investment properties and second homes do not qualify
  • One cash-out refi per year — once closed, you must wait 12 months to do another
  • 12-day waiting period — must wait 12 days after signing the application before closing
  • Specific disclosure forms — Texas requires its own disclosure documents at application
  • Fee cap — lender fees cannot exceed 2% of the loan amount
