Refinance Options

Four ways Terrell homeowners refinance

Rate/Term Refinance

Lower your interest rate, shorten your loan term, or both. Best for homeowners who bought or last refinanced when rates were higher. I calculate your break-even point — the month when savings exceed closing costs — before you decide. At Terrell's price range, closing costs are modest, which often shortens the break-even window.

Cash-Out Refinance (Texas 50(a)(6))

Access your home equity for renovations, debt consolidation, or any purpose. Texas law caps cash-out refis at 80% LTV — stricter than most states. On a Terrell home appraised at $260,000, you could access up to $208,000 minus your current loan balance. Note: USDA loans cannot do cash-out refinancing — only rate/term or the USDA streamline program.

USDA Streamline Refinance

Terrell is USDA-eligible. If you already have a USDA loan, the streamline refinance program lets you refinance with no appraisal and reduced documentation — as long as the new loan lowers your payment. No equity required, and the process is simpler than a standard refi. This is one of the most underutilized refinance programs in the Kaufman County market.

VA IRRRL (Interest Rate Reduction Refinance Loan)

Veterans and active-duty homeowners with an existing VA loan can streamline into a lower rate with no appraisal and no income verification in most cases. The new rate must be lower than your current VA rate (except ARM to fixed). I process VA IRRRLs regularly for Terrell area veterans.
