VA IRRRL — The Streamline Refinance

Fort Worth veterans: the fastest refinance available

The VA Interest Rate Reduction Refinance Loan — known as the IRRRL or "streamline" — is available exclusively to veterans and active-duty homeowners who already have a VA loan. Fort Worth's large military and veteran community near Naval Air Station JRB Fort Worth makes this one of the most in-demand refinance programs I work with in Tarrant County.

  • No appraisal required in most cases
  • No income verification for most IRRRL applications
  • Must lower your rate (or move from ARM to fixed)
  • VA funding fee: 0.5% (waived if 10%+ service-connected disability)
  • Must be current on payments — no 30-day lates in last 12 months
  • Minimum 210 days since first payment on existing VA loan
