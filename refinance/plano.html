FHA-to-Conventional Refinance

Plano FHA borrowers: you may be able to drop MIP permanently

FHA loans require mortgage insurance for the life of the loan when you put less than 10% down — it never cancels automatically. But if your Plano home has appreciated enough that your loan-to-value is now below 80%, you can refinance from FHA into a conventional loan and eliminate MIP entirely.

The monthly savings on a Plano home can be significant. On a $380,000 FHA balance, the MIP is approximately $266/month at the standard 0.85% rate. A conventional refi at 80% LTV carries zero PMI. Even if the rate is similar, removing $266/month in MIP means the refinance pays for itself in closing costs within 15–18 months.
