USDA Home Loans

USDA Loans for Eligible Texas Communities

If you’re buying in an eligible rural or suburban Texas area, a USDA loan can offer some of the most affordable terms available — including 100% financing for qualified borrowers.

Why USDA?

Affordable financing for eligible areas

USDA loans are backed by the U.S. Department of Agriculture to promote homeownership in eligible rural and suburban communities. Many growing Texas towns qualify — and many buyers are surprised to learn their target neighborhood is eligible.

  • Up to 100% financing
  • No down payment for qualified borrowers
  • Affordable monthly payments
  • Designed for eligible rural and suburban communities
  • Income limits and area eligibility apply

Is my area eligible?

Not just farmland

USDA-eligible areas extend well beyond rural farms. Many fast-growing Texas towns and suburbs qualify. Bond Peter will check your target address against current USDA maps.

Eligible suburbs

Income limits apply

FAQ

USDA loan questions

Do I need to live in farmland for a USDA loan?
No. USDA-eligible areas include many suburbs and small towns. Eligibility is based on USDA’s map of qualifying locations.
Is there really no down payment?
For qualified borrowers, USDA loans can offer up to 100% financing. Eligibility, income limits, and area requirements apply.
Are USDA loans only for first-time buyers?
No. USDA loans are available to repeat buyers as well, as long as the property and borrower qualify.
What about closing costs?
Closing costs apply. Some costs may be paid by the seller or rolled into the loan. Bond will explain what to expect.
