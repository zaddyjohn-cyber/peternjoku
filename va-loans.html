VA Home Loans

VA Loans for Texas Veterans and Military Families

You served. Now let your service work for your family. VA loans offer favorable terms for eligible Veterans, active-duty service members, and surviving spouses.

Favorable terms designed around service

VA loans are backed by the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs and offered through approved lenders. The benefits are designed to make homeownership more accessible for those who served — and for many qualified borrowers, that means buying with little to no money down.

  • No down payment for qualified borrowers
  • No monthly mortgage insurance
  • Competitive interest rates
  • Flexible qualification guidelines
  • Reusable benefit for eligible Veterans

FAQ

VA loan questions

Do I need to be a first-time buyer to use a VA loan?
No. The VA loan benefit can often be reused by eligible borrowers under certain conditions.
Are there closing costs with a VA loan?
Yes. Some costs may be paid by the seller or rolled into the loan. I'll walk you through what to expect.
Is the VA funding fee required?
Most VA borrowers pay a funding fee. Certain borrowers may be exempt. I'll confirm based on your file.
Can a VA loan be used for a refinance?
Yes. VA refinance options exist for eligible homeowners.
