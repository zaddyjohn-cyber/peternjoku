Dallas DHAP

Up to $60,000 forgivable — Dallas's flagship DPA program

The Dallas Down Payment Assistance Program is among the most generous city-level DPA programs in Texas. For first-time buyers purchasing within Dallas city limits, DHAP can fund both the down payment and closing costs — on a $300,000 FHA purchase, the buyer's out-of-pocket cost can be $0.

  • Up to $60,000 in assistance
  • Forgivable over 5 years — 20% forgiven each year
  • First-time homebuyer required (or not owned in 3+ years)
  • Property must be within Dallas city limits
  • Income limit: ~$97,200 (Dallas County, 2026)
  • Pairs with FHA, conventional, VA, and USDA loans

How I stack programs for Dallas buyers

My standard approach for a Dallas first-time buyer at under $97,200 income: start with FHA (580+ credit, 3.5% down), apply Dallas DHAP to cover the down payment and closing costs, and layer TSAHC on top if DHAP is fully subscribed. The result is often a buyer who puts zero down and pays minimal closing costs on a $250,000–$380,000 Dallas home.
