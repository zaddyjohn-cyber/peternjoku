Why conventional beats FHA at Flower Mound price points

FHA's lifetime mortgage insurance premium is manageable on a $260K home but adds up on a $450K purchase — roughly $320/month that never goes away. TSAHC pairs with conventional loans, so a Flower Mound buyer can get a 3% TSAHC grant on a conventional loan and drop the MIP entirely once they hit 20% equity.
