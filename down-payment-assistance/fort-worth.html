Fort Worth HAP Deep Dive

The Fort Worth Homebuyer Assistance Program — what buyers need to know

The Fort Worth HAP is a forgivable loan — not a grant — meaning it's recorded as a second lien on your property but forgiven over 5 years at 20% per year. If you sell or refinance before year 5, you repay the prorated remaining balance. If you stay, you owe nothing.

HAP covers down payment and closing costs on homes within Fort Worth city limits. Combined with FHA (3.5% down), a Fort Worth buyer earning under $97,200 can enter a $220,000–$280,000 home with minimal out-of-pocket expenses.
