Why USDA Wins in Royse City

No down payment and no monthly MIP — best deal available

USDA is the strongest mortgage product available to most Royse City buyers. Unlike FHA, USDA has no monthly mortgage insurance premium — just a small annual fee (~0.35% of the loan) that is a fraction of FHA's 0.85% MIP. On a $260K home, that's roughly $110/month less. Over 10 years, that's $13,200 in savings with no down payment required.
